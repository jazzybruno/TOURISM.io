Akagera National Park

This is a park that is found in the eastern part of Rwanda
The largest of all parks in Rwanda it contains many physical features

The physical features include:

  1. Lakes
  2. Savanah glasslands
  3. Forests
  4. Animals

The animals found in the Akagera National Park include:

  1. Lions
  2. Zebras
  3. Elephants
  4. Antelops
  5. Baboons
  6. Giraffes
