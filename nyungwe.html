Nyungwe National Park

This is the park that is found in the southern and western part of Rwanda
This is a natural forest that contains several animals in it.

Animals found in the Nyungwe National park include:

  1. Baboons
  2. Golden monkey
  3. Monkeys
  4. All types of birds
  5. Small insects
  6. Natural Plants
